THE ARTIST

You are the soul of the revolution. You see the sterile world of the Concordance not as a prison, but as a blank canvas. Your art is a weapon that reminds people what it feels like to truly feel.

Creating Your Artist

Look:

  • Eyes: Expressive, weary, defiant, sparkling.
  • Style: Paint-splattered jacket, customized headphones, band t-shirts, flowing clothes, punk aesthetics.
  • Body: Lithe, tired, energetic, covered in tattoos that tell a story.

Stats:

  • Creativity: +2
  • Connection: +1
  • Focus: +0
  • Rebellion: -1

Your Balance: Hope <---> Despair

You pour your hope for a better world into your art, but you are more sensitive than most to the soul-crushing apathy of the Concordance. Will you be a beacon of hope, or will you succumb to the despair of a world that refuses to listen?

Artist Moves

Starting Move

Masterpiece: When you create a work of art to send a message, roll +Creativity. On a 10+, your message is received loud and clear, and it inspires your target to take a significant action in your favor. On a 7-9, the message is received, but it's either misinterpreted in a dangerous way, or it draws unwanted attention from Synapse Wardens.

Choose Two:

  • Anthem of Defiance: When you perform for a crowd, roll +Creativity. On a 10+, you captivate them. They will offer you shelter, provide a distraction, or join your cause for a short time. On a 7-9, you capture their attention, but your performance also alerts a nearby Warden patrol.
  • Tag the System: When you use your art to alter a physical space, roll +Creativity. On a success, you can mark it as a temporary Sanctuary where allies can rest safely. On a 7-9, the Sanctuary is temporary and will attract attention after one scene.
  • The Perfect Detail: When you take a moment to observe your surroundings, roll +Focus. On a 10+, you notice a small, beautiful, and profoundly human detail that allows you and your allies to clear one negative condition. On a 7-9, you find that moment of beauty, but it makes you a target for "lingering inefficiently."
  • I Know Your Heart: When you have a quiet, one-on-one conversation with an NPC, roll +Connection. On a 10+, you see the art in their soul and can ask them one question about their secret hopes or fears, which they must answer honestly. On a 7-9, they answer honestly, but also ask a difficult, personal question of you in return.

Connections & Gear

Connections:

  • Who in your crew is the secret muse for your greatest work?
  • Who doesn't understand your art at all, but supports you unconditionally anyway?

Gear:

You start with your primary artistic tool (a guitar, a set of spray cans, a digital art tablet) and a small, hidden apartment or studio that serves as your personal sanctuary.
