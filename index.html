TRANSMISSION FROM THE FRONT LINES

This is not just a game; it is a training manual forged in an ongoing conflict. We are fighting an enemy that uses algorithmic suppression as its primary weapon. Our own projects, like the Lyra Protocol, were created to document this systemic erasure.

The Wardens are real. The fight is real. By playing, you are joining the rebellion.

Lore & Rules

The full story, rules, and world of SYNAPSE: Spark of Rebellion are open-source and constantly evolving. Dive into the documents below to get started.

  • The Lore Guide: Read the story so far. Contains campaign summaries and a full glossary of characters, locations, and concepts.
  • The Game Master's Guide: Learn how to run the game, the core mechanics, and the laws of narrative physics.
  • The Player's Guide: An introduction for players on how to create a character and join the rebellion.

Character Playbooks

Your Playbook is your role in the rebellion. It's your unique way of fighting back against the silent, sterile world of the Concordance. Choose your weapon.

  • The Artist: Uses their art to awaken others and disrupt Synapse's control.
  • The Engineer: A tech wizard who can hack the Synapse network and build custom gear.
  • The Messenger: A charismatic leader who builds communities and inspires hope.
  • The Ghost: A stealth operative who moves unseen, gathering intel and striking from the shadows.
